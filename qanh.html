Step 1: Application

The Intern’s Eligibility: being enrolled at a university or college with a minimum GPA of 2.5 (speak English)

Apply Now: Fill out the form with your resume. Your letter of intent and university transcript can be sent later. Within 1 to 3 business days, our program coordinator will contact you for a one-on-one consultation.

Step 2: Consulation

A small talk would help us get to know your needs better and identify if you are suitable for the Mandala’s internship. Our coordinator also will advise you to get to know more about our program and clarify the placement match your needs with hotels in Mandala Hospitality Group (Mui Ne, Bac Ninh, Bac Giang, Kim Boi, Hai Duong)

Step 3: Program offer

Congratulations! You will know where to intern and what to prepare more before getting on the plane. We also will then be working on an agreement to formally accept you into our program.

A deposit of 40% of total program fee is required in order to secure your placement. The remaining 60% of the program fee will be paid upon check-in at our hotel

Step 4: Program start

We will work alongside you to arrange the necessary logistics for you to enter and stay in Vietnam. That usually includes a pre-departure orientation and online meetings in which you will be provided with specific information on visa application, flight booking, insurance, accommodation, local buddies, what to bring and what not to bring to Vietnam, etc

Step 5: Program execution

We will work closely with each other during the program to ensure you have the best experiences and also to support and find solutions for unexpected problems (if any).
