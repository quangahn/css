    INTERNSHIP CONTENT
    The intern will work mainly on the Mandala hotel and assist the hotel manager with the following tasks
    Housekeeping
    Training in housekeeping methods and procedures
    Cleanliness standard, room set up and bedding
    Allocate work duties to Team Members
    Work with supervisor to perform routine inspections of all check out rooms and spot checks of all occupied rooms
    Report and follow up on any maintenance defects or other issues
    Manage, efficiently, stock control and the maintenance of equipment
    Front Office
    Introduction to front desk and meet & greet procedures and complaint handling
    Perform check in/out, hotel operator and meet & greet
    Familiarization on night audit
    Shadow team on daily operations
    Support on guest services and advice on transportation, business services or entertainment, or monitor guest requests for housekeeping and maintenance
    Food and Beverage
    Practice F&B service sequence and basic techniques
    Job rotation to experience all positions within F&B team
    Shadow F&B Manager on daily operations
    Check Function forecast to arrange member logically
    Build a equipment, materials plan for FB department weekly
    Ensure the manager is informed of special events, house counts and noteworthy situations
    Spa
    Provide personalized supports to regular guests to ensure they are well taken care of
    Handle guest requests and concerns promptly and report to the Spa Manager
    Assist for the day to day spa operation duties to include opening and closing, cash handling procedures and general facility maintenance
    Learn to build a plan for entertainment activities, menu and promotion campaign
    Operation Management
    Learn to provide timely and thoughtful guidance, coaching, leadership and expert support to all departments
    Ensure consistent compliance with hotel policies and outstanding, intuitive guest service while maximizing departmental profits
    Proactively and thoughtfully anticipate and prevent negative guest experiences through training; communication; planning and organizing
    Oversee all projects within the property; work closely with the Head Office; suppliers and the community to enhance the guest experience and workplace environment
